Online should mean co-working, not surveillance.

A future member mode where builders can enter the network remotely, share a lightweight session context, and get useful help while they are actually working.

01

Live work session

A builder can mark themselves active, add what they are working on, and optionally stream, screen-share, or post a short session note.

02

Context, not intrusion

The useful unit is current project, blocker, and ask. Presence and work details stay opt-in and can be private by default.

03

Mid-work intros

When two active builders have overlapping projects, asks, or skills, the system can suggest a low-friction intro at the right moment.

04

Profile approval

Public profiles start with demo people, then this month's builders, then checked-in members, each confirmed by email before publishing.
